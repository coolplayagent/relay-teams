# hello source archive

This note records how `/opt/workspace/hello` Markdown sources referenced by
`docs/research/lessons-learned-2026.md` were redistributed into topic-specific
research directories.

Most files were grouped by research topic instead of preserving the old
the former hello archive tree. For example,
`hello source: coding-agent-orchestration/openai-symphony-research.md`
now lives at `docs/research/coding-agent-orchestration/openai-symphony-research.md`.

Selection scope:

- Coding agent collaboration and orchestration platform research.
- OpenAI Symphony, Codex harness, sandbox, realtime eval, and WebSocket workflow notes.
- 2026 Harness, SDD, MAS, Google, market, presentation, and Chinese research summary entry points used by the analysis.
- SPDD material under `spdd/`, including the REASONS Canvas workflow, OpenSPDD CLI notes, and SDD ecosystem comparison.
- Formal verification research under `formal-verification/`, including the research report and archived PDF references.
- Root AI-native organization playbook used for governance and handoff lessons.

Repository style normalization:

- Source status glyphs and presentation pictograms were converted to text labels in the archived copies.
- The original content structure and relative path layout were otherwise preserved for reviewability.
